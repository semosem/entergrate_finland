Connecting communities for a more inclusive Finland.

At Entergrate, we are committed to supporting immigrants by expanding our outreach and impact through our 'In the Spotlight' events. Together, we can create a welcoming Finland where immigrants can thrive both personally and professionally.

Find Out More

Entergrate Community in Finland

Entergrate is a community that strives to facilitate the integration of immigrants into society. Our mission is to promote professional and social inclusiveness for immigrants in Finland, enabling them to contribute to the society and enrich their own lives. Our members consist of both working professionals and volunteers who form a network connecting immigrants with relevant organizations.

We strongly believe in promoting networking and creating synergies between various entities serving immigrant communities, locals, and immigrant individuals.

By establishing connections and bringing people together, we aim to ease the challenges immigrants encounter during their entry into the labor market and in fitting into the society.
